Accessibility notes (LIT-74)

Import order: tokens.css → components.css → a11y.css. Every page needs a skip link, one h1, landmark regions, and aria-current="page" on the active nav item (underline + weight, not color alone).

Stakes / alert copy uses darkened coral (--ll-color-stakes-text) for AA on Mist Paper. Raw Ember Coral is large text or decorative only.

Tab through buttons and nav to verify dual-tone focus rings. Toggle OS “Reduce motion” to confirm hover lifts stop.
